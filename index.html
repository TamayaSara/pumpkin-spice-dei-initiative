ALPACOMM's purpose is to help and support all staff to imbue their substantial identities and cultures within our company. We create a safe space in order to allow every individual the chance to share their specialized knowledge with The Alpaca Company. The website creates a place where prejudice can be overcome through our educational events listed on the ERG page as well as a space where you can safely address harassment and discrimination. ALPACOMM is a platform to aid new employees that are new to the company by using ERGs and mentorships to help onboard them and feel at home within the company.

Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

At The Alpaca Company, we want to provide the best for the next generations. With each grade, comes many new personalities, cultures, and different faces. We at The Alpaca Company find it crucial to have a representative staff for these children to show the diverse youth of NYC that they can do anything. With this ALPACOMM platform, we hope to reaffirm our commitment to maintaining our diverse staff. To supplement the richness of quality that our diverse staff brings, we strive to create an environment of equal standing for what each of our culture's knowledge can bring to the company.

We welcome people from all backgrounds and beliefs to get involved with The Alpaca Company. We will come together to pioneer a path for the next generation. Our company seeks people from different communities and groups to show that working and having productive discussions with each other is possible. Together with everyone in the group and their knowledge, the educational games we create will be beyond what one community or group can ever think of.

Diversity

Equity

We understand that people of marginalized and minority communities often face discrimination in the tech industry and at The Alpaca Company, we hope to combat this by initiatives like this platform. We do our best to treat our employees equally and invite employees to share any issues that may arise with us so that we can ensure we maintain this environment. Together with many groups, we can remove the prejudices against each other to create a better environment for the next group.

At The Alpaca Company, we create opportunities to include everyone from all identities and groups. We promise to present many chances for everyone to be included in situations. We strive to create an environment where every individual employee feels like the essential part of our mission that they are. We also hope to provide the space for employees to gain a sense of togetherness and belonging at the company.
