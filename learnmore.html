Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

At Project Alpaca, we want to provide the best for our new generations. With each grade, comes many new personalities, cultures, and different faces. We at Project Alpaca find it crucial to have a representative staff for these children to show the diverse youth of NYC that they can do anything. We at Project Alpaca are reaffirming our commitment to maintaining our diverse staff. To provide the richness of quality that our diverse staff brings, we create an environment of equal standing for what each of our culture's knowledge can bring to the company.
